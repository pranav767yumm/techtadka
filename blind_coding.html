About the Event

Blind Coding is the ultimate test of your programming syntax and memory. Participants must write error-free code to solve a given problem while their monitors are turned off! It's not just about what you know, but how well you know your keyboard.

Round Details

  • Round 1: Simple Syntax Challenge (Monitor ON).
  • Round 2: Intermediate Logic (Monitor OFF).
  • Round 3: Advanced Algorithms (Monitor OFF).

Rules & Guidelines

1. Participants must solve the problem in C, C++, Java, or Python.

2. No monitor use allowed during the specific "Blind" rounds.

3. Winner is decided based on code accuracy and compilation success.
